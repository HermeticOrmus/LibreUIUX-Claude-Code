## Troubleshooting Combinations

### Feels Too Serious
Add: Fool, Jester, Sun cards
Try: Current+Sun or Jester archetype blend

### Feels Too Playful
Add: Hermit, Emperor, Justice cards
Try: Current+Hermit or Ruler archetype blend

### Lacks Energy
Add: Sun, Chariot, Tower cards
Try: Hero or Rebel archetype blend

### Too Intense
Add: Star, Temperance, Innocent cards
Try: Caregiver or Sage archetype blend

### No Emotional Impact
Question: What should users *feel*?
Then: Choose archetype for behavior, card for emotion

### Colors Fight Structure
- Check if card enhances or contradicts archetype
- Example: Rebel+Temperance conflicts (chaos vs. balance)
- Solution: Choose complementary pairing

## Memorization Tricks

### Archetypes
Think of famous brands:
- **Hero**: Nike, Under Armour (achievement)
- **Sage**: Wikipedia, TED (knowledge)
- **Innocent**: Dove, Innocent Drinks (safety)
- **Explorer**: Jeep, Patagonia (adventure)
- **Rebel**: Harley Davidson, Virgin (disruption)

### Cards
Think of the symbolism:
- **Sun**: Bright yellow, midday, celebration
- **Moon**: Silver blue, night, dreams
- **Tower**: Lightning strike, chaos
- **Star**: Night sky, hope, guidance
- **Hermit**: Lantern in darkness, solitude
